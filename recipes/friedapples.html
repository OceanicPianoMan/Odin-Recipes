Fried Apples

Description

These copycat Cracker Barrel fried apples feature Golden Delicious apples, simmered in a buttery, sweer sauce until tender, and then thickened with a constarch slurry

Ingredients

  • 6 tablespoons butter
  • 4 Golden delicious apples, sliced, or more as needed
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons constarch

Steps:

  1. Melt Butter in a deep skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add apples, cinnamon and sugar until well coated in sugar mixture
  3. Bring mixture to a simmer
  4. Reduce heat to a medium-low and cook, covered until apples are very tender (20 minutes)
  5. Whisk together cold water and cornstartch until dissolved
  6. Stir cornstarch mixture into apple mixture and return to a simmer
  7. Cook until thickend, about 2 minutes
  8. Remove from heat

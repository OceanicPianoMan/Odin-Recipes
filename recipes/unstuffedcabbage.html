Unstuffed Cabbage

Description

An unstuffed cabbage roll is a deconstructed version of a regular casserole. All the traditional cabbage roll ingredients (cabbage, ground beef, tomatoes, other veggies, and spices) are cooked together — no need to pre-cook or roll the cabbage.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 1 small head cabbage, chopped
  • 2 (14.5 oz) cans diced tomatoes
  • 1 (8 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Heat a Dutch Oven or large skillet over medium-high heat
  3. Cook and stir beef and onion until browned and crumbly (5–7 minutes)
  4. Drain and discard grease
  5. Add cabbage, tomatoes, tomato sauce, water, garlic, salt, and pepper; bring to a boil
  6. Cover, reduce heat, and simmer until cabbage is tender (30 minutes)
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!

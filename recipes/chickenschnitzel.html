Chicken Schnitzel

Description

Think you can't make chicken schnitzel without frying? Think again! This baked recipe imitates that deep-fried feel without using a ton of oil. All you need is to employ a couple simple hacks â€” heat the pan beforehand and drizzle the chicken with olive oil twice.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil, or as desired
  • 6 chicken breasts, cut in galf lengthwise (butterflied)
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon ground paprika
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1 large lemon, zested

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 425F or 220C
  3. Line a large baking sheet with aluminum foil and drizzle olive oil over foil
  4. Place the baking sheet in the oven as it preheats
  5. Place 2 chicken breasts between 2 sheets of plastic wrap on a solid, level surface
  6. Firmly pound with the smooth side of a meta mallet to 1/4-inch thickness
  7. Season both sides with salt and pepper
  8. Mix flour and paprike together in a shallow bowl
  9. Whisk eggs, salt, and pepper together in a second shallow bowl
  10. Mix bread crumbs and lemon zest together in a third shallow bowl
  11. Dredge one piece of chicken in flour; shake off excess
  12. Dip into beaten egg
  13. Lift up so excess egg drips back in the bowl
  14. Press into bread crumbs to coat both sides
  15. Place the breaded chicken, unstacked, onto a plate
  16. Repeat with remaining chicken
  17. Remove the baking sheet from the oven
  18. Arrange chicken in a single layer on the hot sheet
  19. Drizzle more olive oil over each piece
  20. Bake in the preheated oven for 5 to 6 minutes
  21. Flip chicken and continue baking until no longer pink in the center and breading is lightly browned (5 to 6 minutes)
  22. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C)
  23. Enjoy!

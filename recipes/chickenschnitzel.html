Chicken Schnitzel

Description

Think you can't make chicken schnitzel without frying? Think again! This baked recipe imitates that deep-fried feel without using a ton of oil. Heat the pan beforehand and drizzle the chicken with olive oil twice.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil, or as desired
  • 6 chicken breasts, cut in half lengthwise (butterflied)
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon ground paprika
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1 large lemon, zested

Steps:

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Preheat oven to 425째F / 220째C
  3. Line a large baking sheet with aluminum foil and drizzle olive oil over foil
  4. Place the baking sheet in the oven as it preheats
  5. Place 2 chicken breasts between 2 sheets of plastic wrap on a solid, level surface
  6. Firmly pound with the smooth side of a meat mallet to 1/4-inch thickness
  7. Season both sides with salt and pepper
  8. Mix flour and paprika together in a shallow bowl
  9. Whisk eggs, salt, and pepper together in a second shallow bowl
  10. Mix bread crumbs and lemon zest together in a third shallow bowl
  11. Dredge one piece of chicken in flour; shake off excess
  12. Dip into beaten egg
  13. Press into bread crumbs to coat both sides
  14. Place the breaded chicken, unstacked, onto a plate
  15. Repeat with remaining chicken
  16. Remove the baking sheet from the oven
  17. Arrange chicken in a single layer on the hot sheet
  18. Drizzle more olive oil over each piece
  19. Bake for 5-6 minutes, flip, and continue baking until no longer pink and breading is lightly browned
  20. Check internal temperature (165째F / 74째C)
  21. Enjoy!
